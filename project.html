Project 7: Online Exam System with Camera Proctoring for VIVA College using PHP


The Online Exam System with Camera Proctoring for VIVA College is an advanced web-based application developed using PHP. It offers a comprehensive platform for conducting online exams and assessments while integrating camera proctoring capabilities to ensure exam integrity and prevent cheating.


Key Features:

  • Secure and Reliable: The online exam system prioritizes the security and reliability of exam data, providing a safe environment for students to take exams without compromising the integrity of the assessment process.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The system features a user-friendly interface that is intuitive and easy to navigate, offering a seamless exam experience for students.
  • Flexible Exam Creation: Faculty members have the flexibility to create exams with various question types and customize them according to their requirements, allowing for a comprehensive evaluation of student knowledge.
  • Camera Proctoring: The system incorporates camera proctoring functionality, using the webcam to monitor students during exams and detect any suspicious activities to maintain exam integrity.
  • Automated Grading: The system automates the grading process, providing instant and accurate results, saving time for faculty members, and ensuring fair evaluation for students.
  • Exam Analytics: The system generates comprehensive analytics and reports, allowing faculty members to analyze exam results, identify patterns, and gain insights to improve the assessment process.

The Online Exam System with Camera Proctoring for VIVA College offers a secure and advanced platform for conducting online exams, combining innovative technology with robust features to ensure the integrity of the assessment process and promote fair evaluation.
